ApplicativeLayerFunctor 

Companion object ApplicativeLayerFunctor

trait ApplicativeLayerFunctor[M[_], Inner[_]] extends ApplicativeLayer[M, Inner] with FunctorLayerFunctor[M, Inner] with Serializable

ApplicativeLayerFunctor is the capability to lift Applicative homomorphisms in Inner (Inner ~> Inner) into homomorphisms in M (Inner ~> Inner).

ApplicativeLayerFunctor[M, Inner] has no additional laws.
